Sophia Linarez

Digital Media Artist

Sophia Linarez is a digital artist based in the Bay Area. She creates her projects through digital art and coding to convey her message. When she isn't creating art she indulges in her other hobbies such as games and films, where she draws some inspiration for her own personal work. Her art is a reflection of who she is as a person and as an artist. Through her work Sophia hopes to pass along a meaningful message that will evoke a feeling with her audience in the hopes they will also find her work enjoyable and relatable.
